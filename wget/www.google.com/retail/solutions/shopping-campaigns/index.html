Benefits to your business

Get on Google, get results.

Shopping ads are more than just a text ad–they show users a photo of your product, plus a title, price, store name, and more. Use shopping campaigns to promote your online and local inventory, boost traffic to your website or local store, and find better qualified leads.

Show up across Google.

Shopping ads appear on Google Search, Images, YouTube, and more, when people search for products you sell.

Appear on multiple devices.

Reach your customers, whether they’re shopping at home or on the go.

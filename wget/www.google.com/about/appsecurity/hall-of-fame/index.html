What factors influence the ranking?

  • Volume: The more valid bug reports you've sent in, the more points you get. Points are deducted for spurious reports.
  • Severity: For those bugs, how severe are they? Better bugs earn more points.
  • Recency: The most recent bug reports receive maximum value. Bug reports older than 6 months lose an increasing proportion of their value over time, capped at 50%.
  • Charity: If you give, you receive a bump to your score.

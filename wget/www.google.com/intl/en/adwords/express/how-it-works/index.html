How AdWords Express delivers results.

Getting started is easy and only takes about 15 minutes.

1 Create your ad.

Start by writing an ad that tells people what you offer. Next, choose which business category best describes you and set your monthly budget. That’s it — your ad is ready to go live.

2 People see your ad on Google.

When people search for what you offer, your ad can appear above or below the search results.

3 You get more customers.

When people click your ad, they’ll see your website or your free Google My Business page. They can also call your business directly. Best of all, you’ll only pay when people click or call.
